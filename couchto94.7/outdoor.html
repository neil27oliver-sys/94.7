Quick Note on Screen Sleep:

To preserve your battery during long rides, most phones automatically turn off the screen after about 5 minutes of inactivity.

To bring your live data back: Just tap anywhere on the screen – it wakes instantly!

Pro tip: In your phone Settings → Display → Screen timeout, you can extend this to 10, 15, or 30 minutes for uninterrupted viewing during outdoor sessions. (Remember to reset it afterward to save power.)
