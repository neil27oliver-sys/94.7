PHYSICAL TRAINING

Progressive 12-Week Program – From Couch to 94.7 km Endurance

Program Overview

Sustainable build: 5 training days/week (4 indoor/virtual + 1 outdoor weekend). Leave 2 full rest/recovery days. Focus on FTP progression, power and off-bike strength.

  • Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–4) – Build base. Target ~4:30 hr effort. Indoor rides 40–50 min.
  • Phase 2: Build (Weeks 5–8) – Add intensity/endurance. Target ~4:00 hr effort. Indoor rides 50–60 min.
  • Phase 3: Race Prep (Weeks 9–12) – Peak power & speed. Target ~3:30 hr effort. Indoor rides 50–60+ min.
Key Rules:
- Re-test FTP every 4 weeks (end of phase) to unlock next level.
- Pair indoor days with off-bike strength 2–3x/week.
- Practice race fueling on long weekend rides.
- Daily stretching exercises enhance flexibility, improve range of motion, reduce injury risk, and alleviate stiffness.

Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

Build basic strength, stability & injury resistance. Target: ~4:30 hr effort tolerance.

Core Workout (3x/week)

Plank

Hold core tight, body straight.

3 × 20-30s | Rest 60s

Bird Dog

On all fours, extend opposite arm/leg.

3 × 10 per side | Rest 45s

Legs Workout (2x/week)

Squats

Feet shoulder-width, lower to parallel.

3 × 12 reps | Rest 60s

Lunges

Step forward, alternate legs.

3 × 10 per leg | Rest 60s

Phase 2: Build (Weeks 5–8)

Increase endurance & volume. Target: ~4:00 hr effort. Add bands if available.

Core Workout (3x/week)

Plank

3 × 30-45s | Rest 60s

Dead Bug

3 × 12 per side | Rest 45s

Phase 3: Race Prep (Weeks 9–12)

Peak strength, intensity & power. Target: ~3:30 hr effort.

Legs & Power (2x/week)

Weighted Squats

3 × 20 reps | Add weights/bands

Calf Raises

3 × 25 reps | Rest 45s
