The Anatomy of a Cyclist

Conquering the 94.7km Ride Joburg demands leveraging your body's natural strengths while respecting its vulnerabilities.

Primary Strengths

The Engine: Your muscular and cardiovascular systems. Training builds the "warrior heart" needed to deliver oxygen to your legs during the toughest climbs.

The Core: Your abdominal and lower back muscles provide the stable platform that allows your legs to generate maximum power.

Known Vulnerabilities

Hotspots: Repetitive motion can lead to knee strain or IT Band issues. Knowledge of these "weaknesses" allows you to train smarter, not harder.
