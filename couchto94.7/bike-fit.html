Bike Fit Optimization Guide

Dial your setup for power, comfort, and injury prevention

1. Frame Size Basics

Rough guide by height: 160-170cm → 51-54cm frame, etc. Check manufacturer charts.

2. Saddle Height (LeMond Method)

Inseam (cm) × 0.883 = BB center to saddle top.
On-bike: 25-35° knee bend at bottom stroke.

3. Saddle Fore/Aft – KOPS Rule Explained

Cranks horizontal (3 o'clock). Drop plumb line from tibial tuberosity (bump below kneecap).
Line should pass over pedal spindle center.
Note: Great starting point, but not gospel—fine-tune by feel (no pain, stable hips).

4. Handlebar Reach & Drop

Elbows slightly bent, flat back, relaxed neck. Bars 0-10cm below saddle.

5. Cleat Position & Common Fixes

Ball of foot over axle. Adjust if hot spots or knee tracking issues.

Pro Tip: Make one change at a time (2-5mm). Test on short rides. If pain persists → professional fit.
VO2 Max Bonus: A dialed fit improves oxygen efficiency and power output—your intervals will hit harder!
